# Restaurant Management System (RMS) - Online Food Ordering & Delivery

A full-featured Restaurant Management System that allows users to browse menus, place orders online, and have food delivered directly to their location. Built with a focus on usability, efficiency, and a smooth customer experience.

---

## üåü Features

- **User Registration & Login:** Secure authentication for customers.  
- **Menu Management:** View available dishes with details, prices, and images.  
- **Order Placement:** Add items to cart, place orders, and track order status.  
- **Delivery Management:** Integration of delivery tracking for users.  
- **Admin Dashboard:** Manage menu items, orders, and view sales reports.  
- **Responsive Design:** Works seamlessly on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.  
- **Notifications:** Email or in-app notifications for order updates.
